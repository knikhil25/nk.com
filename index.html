About Me

I’m a funny, curious human whose sense of humor sometimes goes into overdrive—and I love solving problems. That could mean solving a Rubik’s Cube, debugging my code, or convincing myself I’ll win the next Mario Kart 8 Deluxe race (spoiler: sometimes I actually do). I can code on my own, but I also enjoy teaming up with AI platforms like Antigravity, because coding with AI feels like having a superpower… or at least a very smart sidekick who never gets tired.


When I’m not coding or gaming, I’m probably thinking about cars. I love how they look, how they work, and why they’re so cool—even when I’m just reading about them. Speaking of reading, I love books. Give me a good story or an interesting topic, and I’ll happily disappear into it for hours.


Art is also a big part of my life. I enjoy origami, crafting, and turning random ideas into something creative and fun. Overall, I love learning, creating, and having fun while doing it. If there’s a challenge, a game, or a cool idea involved, I’m probably interested and already thinking about how to make it better.


Contact me using my email at the bottom of the page if you want me to work on a project. (JK. I am not even in middle school!)
